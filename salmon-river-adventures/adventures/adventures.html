Our Adventure Options:

White Water Rafting

  • Length: 4 hours or 8 hours
  • Adventure Level: high
  • Cost: $150/person $250/person
  • Description: A high level adventure for half a day or a full day. We provide rafts, life vests, helments, and a 30 minutes required training video.

Zip Lining

  • Length: 1 hour
  • Adventure Level: high
  • Cost: $75
  • Description: Zip line across the widest section of the river! This high level adventure comes with protective gear, an instructional video, and a half mile long ride across the river! You will be bused back.

Kayaking

  • Length: 4 hours or 8 hours
  • Adventure Level: Mild
  • Cost: $50/ person or $80/person
  • Description: Spend a half day or all day kayaking a gentle portion of the river. We provide the kayak and life vest.

Paddle Boarding

  • Length: 4 hours
  • Adventure Level: Moderate
  • Cost: $35/person (additional $25 for 1 hour lesson)
  • Description: Spend half the day paddle boarding a mild portion of the river. We provide a board, and protective gear. For $25 more, we will provide an hour of training.
